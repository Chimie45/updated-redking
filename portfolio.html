Game of Gold

Client: Poker After Dark
Industry: iGaming
Duration: 6 months
Services: Web Development, SEO, Content Strategy

Project Overview

Successfully took over the Poker After Dark brand from PokerGo and spearheaded the complete digital transformation for the Game of Gold season. Created a comprehensive web applet for the revolutionary new season, implementing the Game of Gold brand identity as a hybrid poker-reality TV show. Provided on-location production support during filming in Seoul, South Korea, assisting international talent and coordinating with production teams. Managed the complete global release strategy including social media presence, thumbnail creation, multilingual translations and subtitles, while coordinating closely with QA teams to ensure flawless execution across all platforms.

3M+YouTube Views
485KEpisode 1 Views
13Languages

Results & Impact

Game of Gold became an overnight sensation, amassing over 3 million views on YouTube and revolutionizing the poker viewing experience. The inaugural episode attracted 485,000 viewers in its first six weeks, with strong retention throughout the 12-episode season. The show successfully established a new format combining high-stakes poker with reality TV elements, setting a new standard for poker entertainment and winning industry recognition. The project demonstrated exceptional execution across international production, digital strategy, and global content distribution.
