Consistency matters more than the exact amount of time. Even 15-30 minutes a day can lead to great results if you stick with it.

The three courses are split by level, so you can start wherever fits your game. Many experienced players jump straight into the Advanced & ICM course and use the earlier material as a refresher — the drills and EV scores will quickly show you which spots are worth your time.
