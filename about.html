About RedKing Marketing

Your Trusted Partners in Gaming Marketing Excellence

With years of experience in the gaming and iGaming industries, RedKing Marketing has established itself as a leading force in digital marketing, community building, and strategic growth across global markets.

Who We Are

RedKing Marketing is a specialized digital marketing agency dedicated exclusively to the gaming and iGaming industries. We're not just another marketing agency – we're passionate gamers and industry insiders who understand the unique challenges and opportunities that gaming companies face in today's competitive landscape.

Our team combines deep gaming industry knowledge with cutting-edge marketing expertise to deliver results that matter. From community-driven campaigns to data-driven user acquisition, we speak the language of gaming and know how to connect brands with their target audiences across diverse global markets.

What sets us apart is our genuine understanding of gaming culture, player behavior, and the regulatory complexities of different markets. We don't just create campaigns – we build lasting relationships between brands and their communities, driving authentic engagement that translates into real business growth.

Our Story

The Beginning (2018)

RedKing Marketing was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between innovative gaming companies and their global audiences. Starting with a focus on the Asian gaming market, our founder Thomas Streetman recognized the need for specialized marketing expertise in this rapidly growing industry.

Asian Market Expansion (2019-2020)

We established strong partnerships across Japan, Korea, and Southeast Asia, helping major gaming companies navigate complex cultural and regulatory landscapes. Our deep understanding of Asian gaming markets became our competitive advantage.

Community Building Success (2021-2022)

Our community management expertise gained recognition as we built and managed some of the largest gaming communities globally, including the Lost Ark community ecosystem with over 660,000 combined members across platforms.

Industry Recognition (2023-2024)

Our innovative campaigns for major clients like GGPoker, WPT Global, and Game of Gold earned industry recognition and established RedKing as a trusted partner for high-profile gaming brands worldwide.

Global Leadership (2025-Present)

Today, RedKing Marketing continues to lead the industry with comprehensive marketing solutions, serving clients across Asia, Americas, and global markets with a full-service approach to gaming marketing excellence.
