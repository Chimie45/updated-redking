About RedKing Marketing

Your Trusted Partners in Gaming Marketing Excellence

Founded in 2016, RedKing Marketing has grown into a global leader in gaming and iGaming marketing, delivering data-driven strategies and authentic community-building across diverse markets.

Who We Are

RedKing Marketing is a specialized digital marketing agency dedicated exclusively to the gaming and iGaming industries. We’re passionate gamers and industry insiders who understand the unique challenges and opportunities that gaming companies face.

Our team combines deep industry knowledge with cutting-edge digital marketing expertise to build communities, drive user acquisition, and optimize performance campaigns worldwide.

We speak the language of gaming culture — from Twitch streams to mobile PvP tournaments — and translate it into effective marketing strategies that resonate with players.

Our Story

The Beginning (2016)

Thomas Streetman launched RedKing Marketing to bridge the gap between gaming brands and their global audiences, starting with a focus on the Asian gaming market.

Asian Market Expansion (2019–2020)

We formed key partnerships across Japan, Korea, and Southeast Asia, helping top gaming companies navigate cultural and regulatory challenges.

Community Building Success (2021–2022)

Our community management expertise earned recognition as we nurtured global gaming communities, including over 660,000 members for Lost Ark.

Industry Recognition (2023–2024)

Campaigns for GGPoker, WPT Global, and Game of Gold garnered awards and solidified our reputation among high-profile gaming brands.

Global Leadership (2025–Present)

We continue to lead with full-service solutions, serving clients across Asia, the Americas, and beyond, with a proven track record in gaming marketing excellence.
