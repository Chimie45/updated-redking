We're looking for a motion designer to help us create short, high-impact video ads for use on platforms like Google Ads, Meta, and Twitter. One of these roles might require deep poker knowledge, so please clearly state in your proposal whether or not you're familiar with poker.

What You’ll Do (Responsibilities)

  • Design and animate short-form performance ads in 8, 15, and 30-second formats.
  • Work with our raw footage, logos, and brand assets.

  • Must include a portfolio or reel of relevant ad work. Only applicants with motion design samples will be considered.

Preferences

  • Experience in gaming or iGaming (poker/casino).
  • Fluency in Latin Languages or Asian languages.
